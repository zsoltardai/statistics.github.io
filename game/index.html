Fidesz

Fidesz – Hungarian Civic Alliance is a right-wing national-conservative political party in Hungary.

Democratic Coalition

The Democratic Coalition is a social-liberal political party in Hungary, led by former Prime Minister Ferenc Gyurcsány.

Momentum Movement

Momentum Movement is a centrist Hungarian political party founded in March 2017.

Jobbik

The Movement for a Better Hungary, commonly known as Jobbik, is a Hungarian political party.

MSZP

The Hungarian Socialist Party, known mostly by its acronym MSZP, is a social-democratic political party in Hungary.

LMP

LMP – Hungary's Green Party, between 2009 and 2020: Politics Can Be Different, is a green-liberal political party in Hungary, founded in 2009.
